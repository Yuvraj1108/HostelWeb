Facilities

Wi-fi

Wi-fi with good speed and connectivity is available at hostels.

Washing Machine

A sufficient number of good quality automatic washing machines are available for students.

Water Cooler

Every floor of the hostel has a water cooler, which gives RO+UV purified water.

Indoor Games

  • Carrom
  • Chess
  • Table Tennis

Outdoor Games

  • Basketball
  • Lawn Tennis
  • Volleyball
  • Badminton

Recreational Rooms

  • A Common room is available for all the students for various extra-curricular activities. It has a LED TV with Tata sky connection and having enough number of chairs as in the seating arrangement for the students.
  • A Dance practice room equipped with mirror walls is available in the hostel. The institute has also provided speakers for general purposes like dance and music practice.
  • A Music room with all the necessary instruments is available for the students.

Refrigerator

An all-purpose refrigerator is available for students.

MAINTENANCE
  • Maintenance department of hostel council looks to keep appliances and facilities running smoothly.
  • Students can register their maintenance related complaints and suggestions on the CSMS portal. They are forwarded to the concerned department via hostel council.
Dining Hall
  • We have a common dining hall for boys, girls, and faculties located in the hostel complex at GEC campus.
  • Vegetarian and Non-vegetarian food is cooked and served separately.
  • Jain food is also served as per requirement.
  • General Structure of mess menu consists of four meals, Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks, and Dinner.
  • Mess Menu is decided by Mess council in accordance with the suggestions of students
  • Mess related issues can be filed from CSMS portal, Mess Secretary and General Secretary will look for a quick solution.

IIT Goa Dispensary

Savaikar Hospital

  • IIT Goa is on Panel with Savaikar Hospitals, Ponda.
  • We have quite a well equipped Dispensary at the Campus itself, at just 500M from the Hostels.
  • In case of need and by reference of the Nurse/Doctor at IIT Goa, people of IIT Goa, can visit major hospitals in Goa and get a Refund. We are covered by ICICI Lombard medical insurance. The institute covers most of the medical fees with just about Rs 1000 as premium per semester, as medical fees.
