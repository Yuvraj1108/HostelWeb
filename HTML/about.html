About Hostel

IIT Goa was founded in the year 2016 and is temporarily operating from the Goa Engineering College campus.

  • Students usually consider the stay at hostels as an uneasy and cumbersome part of their life. For those who expect to have obnoxious experience, I wish to tell you, the scene here at IIT Goa is completely in contrast to your thought. The hostels here are newly built with all the necessary amenities expected by a student for his stay in the hostels. Hostel of IIT GOA is surrounded by the beauty of nature comprising of hills and mountains covered with greenery. There is a waterfall located in the close proximity of the hostel which is a place of fascination among the students during the weekends.
  • The hostel of IIT GOA comprises of two blocks and each block has two floors with a total capacity of 320 students considering two per room. Each floor consists of 40 rooms, 10 separate restrooms, 10 bathrooms, 2 washing machines,2 geysers, 2 water coolers, and 2 water purifiers. Apart from all this, there are separate common rooms each equipped with television systems and refrigerators in each block. There are special music and dance room, an indoor games room (including TT, chess and carrom) built for the recreation of students. Also, we have basketball, volleyball, badminton and tennis courts next to the hostel blocks. There is also a gymnasium facility which is currently under development. A dining hall is also located in the hostel having a capacity of around 300 students.
  • Each student is provided with personal almirah, bed and the set of study table and chair(with study lamps). Also, the personal LAN connection is being installed for everyone. Rooms are spacious, aerated and equipped with modern lighting systems
  • IIT Goa is currently constructing two new blocks in the hostel which can accommodate 320 people considering two per room.
