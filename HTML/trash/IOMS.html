Declaration

  • 1. When a student is missing his/her classes on a weekday, even for a single day he/she first needs to take permission from Academics office. Further the office will notify us and the Wardens w.r.t student's out of campus request details. Additionally student is requested to inform the Hall office once the approval is received.
  • 2.When a student is missing his/her classes on a weekend, he /she needs to forward his/her application via Hall Office to the Warden for the approval. Again, if any classes are arranged on a Saturday ( falling in between the travel dates of the student) due to any contingency then institute won't be responsible to arrange additional session for him/her.
  • Additionally, full rebate will be given to the students for a minimum period of 2 days and maximum of 30 days in a year only in case a prior application has been sent to the caterer through Hall Office via email, at least 8 days before the actual leave dates. Student in this case would get a rebate for each day applied for rebate.
  • There is No Academic leave during the semester.
  • If any student who wishes to go on leave then he/she is responsible on their own and the institute does not take any responsibility of that student. The institute will not conduct any additional lectures, labs or quizzes for those missed one.
  • If any student is taking leave on academic/medical grounds then he/she needs to attach the copy of the approval from Academics for perusal.

  • If according to our database, you are under 18 years, an email will be sent to your parents regarding your departure and your parents will be responsible for your journey and away time, no matter what you have selected in 'Person who will take my responsibility'.
  • If you are 18 or older, we will inform your parents only when you select 'My Parents' in the field asking 'Person who will take my responsibility'.
